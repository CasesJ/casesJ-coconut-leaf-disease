     └─ Human-readable copies for debugging


🔄 HOW IT WORKS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ONLINE FARMER (WiFi available):
  Take Photo
    ↓
  OpenVINO Inference (local) ✅
    ↓
  Save to SQLite ✅
    ↓
  Check internet → Connected ✅
    ↓
  Sync to Firebase ✅
    ↓
  Result: Stored in BOTH databases ✅✅

OFFLINE FARMER (No internet):
  Take Photo
    ↓
  OpenVINO Inference (local) ✅ (still works!)
    ↓
  Save to SQLite ✅
    ↓
  Check internet → No connection 🔴
    ↓
  Mark as "Pending" 🔄
    ↓
  [Waiting for connectivity]

BACKGROUND SYNC (every 60 seconds):
  Check internet → Connected ✅
    ↓
  Get pending records
    ↓
  For each record:
    • Attempt Firebase sync
    • Retry up to 5 times with backoff
    • Mark synced if successful
    ↓
  Data now in both databases ✅✅
